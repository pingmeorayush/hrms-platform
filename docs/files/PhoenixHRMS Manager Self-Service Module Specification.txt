1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Manager Self-Service (MSS) Module provides managers with a centralized platform to manage teams, monitor workforce activities, approve requests, conduct performance reviews, plan workforce growth, and make data-driven decisions.
The MSS Portal acts as the operational command center for people managers.

Business Objectives
	•	Improve manager productivity
	•	Reduce HR dependency
	•	Enable faster decision making
	•	Improve workforce visibility
	•	Increase team accountability
	•	Support leadership development

Conclusion
The Manager Self-Service Module provides a comprehensive leadership platform that empowers managers to oversee teams, make informed workforce decisions, manage approvals, develop employees, and improve organizational performance.
The module integrates team management, workforce planning, performance oversight, analytics, succession planning, and AI-powered decision support into a secure, scalable, and enterprise-grade management ecosystem while maintaining compliance, auditability, and tenant isolation.
