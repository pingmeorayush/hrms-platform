1. Platform Overview
Purpose
The Audit & Compliance Platform provides centralized governance, auditability, compliance monitoring, evidence management, regulatory reporting, and risk management capabilities across PhoenixHRMS.
The platform ensures every critical business action is traceable, verifiable, and compliant with organizational, industry, and regulatory requirements.

Business Objectives
	•	Maintain complete auditability
	•	Support regulatory compliance
	•	Improve governance
	•	Reduce compliance risks
	•	Simplify audits
	•	Provide legal defensibility

Conclusion
The Audit & Compliance Platform provides a centralized governance, auditability, compliance, and evidence management framework for PhoenixHRMS.
The platform enables immutable audit trails, regulatory compliance, risk management, legal hold capabilities, retention governance, and audit readiness while maintaining scalability, security, compliance, traceability, and tenant isolation across the entire ecosystem.
