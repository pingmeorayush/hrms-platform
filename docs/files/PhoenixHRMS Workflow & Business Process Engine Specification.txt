1. Platform Overview
Purpose
The Workflow & Business Process Engine provides a centralized platform for designing, executing, monitoring, and governing business processes across PhoenixHRMS.
The Workflow Engine serves as the orchestration layer for all approval processes, task routing, escalations, notifications, and business process automation.

Business Objectives
	•	Standardize business processes
	•	Reduce manual effort
	•	Increase automation
	•	Improve compliance
	•	Improve process visibility
	•	Reduce approval turnaround times
	•	Enable low-code workflow configuration

4. Core Concepts
Workflow
A business process definition.
Example:
Leave Request  ↓  Manager Approval  ↓  HR Approval  ↓  Approved

Workflow Instance
A running workflow execution.

Task
Work assigned to a user.

Stage
A logical step within a workflow.

Decision
An approval or rejection action.

Conclusion
The Workflow & Business Process Engine serves as the orchestration backbone of PhoenixHRMS by providing configurable, scalable, secure, and auditable business process automation capabilities.
The platform enables approval workflows, task routing, SLA management, escalations, notifications, and workflow analytics while maintaining flexibility, compliance, auditability, and tenant isolation.
