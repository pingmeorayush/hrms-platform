1. Platform Overview
Purpose
The RBAC & Authorization Platform provides centralized identity authorization, permission management, access governance, and policy enforcement across PhoenixHRMS.
This platform determines:
	•	Who can access a resource
	•	What actions they can perform
	•	Which data they can see
	•	Which fields they can modify
	•	Which tenants they belong to

Business Objectives
	•	Secure system access
	•	Support enterprise authorization
	•	Enable least-privilege access
	•	Reduce security risks
	•	Simplify permission management
	•	Ensure compliance

Conclusion
The RBAC & Authorization Platform provides a centralized, scalable, secure, and enterprise-grade authorization framework that governs access to all PhoenixHRMS resources.
The platform combines RBAC, ABAC, policy-based controls, field-level security, row-level security, delegated access, and multi-tenant isolation to ensure secure, compliant, and auditable access management across the entire platform.
