1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Attendance Management Module is responsible for recording, validating, monitoring, and analyzing employee attendance.
The module serves as the authoritative source for:
	•	Working hours
	•	Presence
	•	Absence
	•	Overtime
	•	Shift compliance
	•	Payroll attendance calculations

Business Objectives
	•	Accurate attendance tracking
	•	Payroll-ready attendance data
	•	Workforce visibility
	•	Compliance support
	•	Attendance analytics
	•	Reduced manual effort

Conclusion
The Attendance Management Module provides a scalable, secure, and enterprise-grade attendance platform capable of supporting office, remote, hybrid, and field workforces.
It serves as the primary source of attendance truth for payroll, analytics, compliance, workforce planning, and employee productivity management while maintaining strong auditability, configurability, and tenant isolation.
