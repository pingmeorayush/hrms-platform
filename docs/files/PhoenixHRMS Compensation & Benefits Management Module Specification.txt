1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Compensation & Benefits Management Module enables organizations to design, administer, monitor, and optimize employee compensation and benefits programs.
The module manages:
	•	Salary Structures
	•	Compensation Cycles
	•	Merit Increases
	•	Promotions
	•	Bonuses
	•	Incentives
	•	Benefits Programs
	•	Insurance Plans
	•	ESOPs
	•	Rewards & Recognition

Business Objectives
	•	Ensure fair compensation
	•	Improve employee retention
	•	Support performance-based rewards
	•	Enable compensation transparency
	•	Improve budgeting accuracy
	•	Increase workforce engagement

Conclusion
The Compensation & Benefits Management Module provides a comprehensive enterprise-grade platform for managing employee rewards, compensation planning, salary reviews, benefits administration, incentives, recognition, and equity programs.
The module integrates compensation governance, workforce rewards, benefits administration, and total rewards visibility into a unified framework while maintaining compliance, transparency, scalability, auditability, and tenant isolation.
