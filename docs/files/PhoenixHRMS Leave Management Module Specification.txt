1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Leave Management Module is responsible for planning, requesting, approving, tracking, accruing, and reporting employee leave.
The module provides:
	•	Leave policies
	•	Leave requests
	•	Leave balances
	•	Leave approvals
	•	Leave accruals
	•	Carry forward
	•	Encashment
	•	Compliance tracking

Business Objectives
	•	Reduce manual leave administration
	•	Enforce company policies
	•	Improve workforce planning
	•	Integrate with payroll
	•	Ensure legal compliance

Conclusion
The Leave Management Module provides a highly configurable, enterprise-grade leave administration platform capable of supporting diverse organizational policies, compliance requirements, and workforce structures.
The module serves as the authoritative source for leave eligibility, balances, approvals, accruals, carry forwards, encashments, and payroll-impacting leave calculations while maintaining auditability, scalability, and tenant isolation.
