1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Payroll Management Module is responsible for calculating, processing, validating, approving, and distributing employee compensation.
The payroll engine serves as the financial core of PhoenixHRMS.

Business Objectives
	•	Accurate salary calculation
	•	Compliance adherence
	•	Automated payroll processing
	•	Financial transparency
	•	Auditability
	•	Integration with accounting systems

Conclusion
The Payroll Management Module is the financial backbone of PhoenixHRMS.
It provides a highly configurable, compliant, auditable, and scalable payroll platform capable of supporting organizations ranging from small businesses to enterprise-scale workforces.
The module integrates attendance, leave, compensation, taxation, compliance, loans, reimbursements, and final settlements into a unified payroll engine while maintaining security, auditability, accuracy, and tenant isolation.
