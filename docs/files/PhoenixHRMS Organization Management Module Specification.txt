1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Organization Management Module serves as the foundational structure of PhoenixHRMS by defining how companies, legal entities, business units, departments, locations, reporting lines, and workforce structures are organized.
Every employee, payroll record, leave policy, workflow, asset, document, and report ultimately belongs to an organizational structure.
This module provides the master organizational hierarchy used across the entire platform.

Business Objectives
	•	Model enterprise organizational structures
	•	Support multi-company environments
	•	Enable workforce segmentation
	•	Improve governance
	•	Support reporting hierarchies
	•	Enable organizational scalability

Conclusion
The Organization Management Module provides the foundational enterprise structure for PhoenixHRMS by managing companies, legal entities, business units, departments, locations, positions, reporting structures, and workforce hierarchies.
The module enables scalable workforce organization, governance, planning, reporting, and compliance while maintaining flexibility, auditability, security, and tenant isolation across the platform.
