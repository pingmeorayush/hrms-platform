1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Asset Management Module manages organizational assets throughout their complete lifecycle.
The module provides:
	•	Asset Inventory
	•	Asset Assignment
	•	Asset Tracking
	•	Maintenance Management
	•	Asset Audits
	•	Asset Recovery
	•	Exit Clearance
	•	Asset Analytics

Business Objectives
	•	Reduce asset loss
	•	Improve asset visibility
	•	Streamline asset assignments
	•	Improve compliance
	•	Optimize asset utilization
	•	Support audits

Conclusion
The Asset Management Module provides a complete enterprise-grade asset lifecycle management platform capable of managing physical assets, digital assets, software licenses, and operational equipment across the organization.
The module integrates inventory management, assignments, maintenance, audits, depreciation, recovery, and compliance into a unified asset ecosystem while maintaining scalability, security, auditability, and tenant isolation.
