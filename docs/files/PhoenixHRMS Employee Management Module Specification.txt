1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Employee Management Module serves as the central repository for all employee-related information within PhoenixHRMS.
This module manages:
	•	Employee lifecycle
	•	Employee profiles
	•	Organizational hierarchy
	•	Employee documents
	•	Employee bank information
	•	Employee contacts
	•	Employee history
	•	Onboarding
	•	Transfers
	•	Promotions
	•	Offboarding

Business Value
Benefits:
	•	Centralized employee records
	•	Reduced administrative effort
	•	Compliance support
	•	Auditability
	•	Workforce visibility
	•	Accurate downstream payroll processing

Conclusion
The Employee Management Module serves as the foundational domain of PhoenixHRMS and acts as the system of record for all workforce information.
All downstream modules including Attendance, Leave, Payroll, Performance, Assets, Reports, and AI depend on the integrity and accuracy of this module.
The module is designed to support organizations ranging from small businesses to enterprise-scale workforces while maintaining security, auditability, scalability, and compliance.
