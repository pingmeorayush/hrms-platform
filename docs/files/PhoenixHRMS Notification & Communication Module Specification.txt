1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Notification & Communication Module provides centralized messaging, notification delivery, communication tracking, and omnichannel engagement capabilities across the PhoenixHRMS platform.
The module supports:
	•	Email
	•	SMS
	•	WhatsApp
	•	Push Notifications
	•	In-App Notifications
	•	Workflow Communications
	•	Campaign Communications
	•	Communication Analytics

Business Objectives
	•	Improve employee engagement
	•	Ensure timely communication
	•	Centralize notification management
	•	Increase workflow efficiency
	•	Support multichannel delivery
	•	Improve communication analytics

Conclusion
The Notification & Communication Module serves as the communication backbone of PhoenixHRMS, providing a centralized, scalable, and enterprise-grade omnichannel messaging platform.
The module enables workflow automation, employee engagement, compliance communications, campaign management, and communication analytics while maintaining security, auditability, reliability, scalability, and tenant isolation.
