1. Platform Overview
Purpose
The Production Operations Runbook defines the operational procedures, support processes, incident response workflows, monitoring standards, reliability objectives, and disaster recovery procedures required to operate PhoenixHRMS in production environments.
The runbook serves as the operational guide for:
	•	DevOps Teams
	•	SRE Teams
	•	Production Support Teams
	•	Security Teams
	•	Infrastructure Teams
	•	Platform Administrators

Business Objectives
	•	Ensure platform availability
	•	Minimize downtime
	•	Reduce operational risks
	•	Improve incident response
	•	Maintain customer trust
	•	Support enterprise SLAs

Conclusion
The Production Operations Runbook provides the operational foundation required to run PhoenixHRMS as a mission-critical enterprise SaaS platform.
The runbook defines incident management, monitoring, disaster recovery, production support, security operations, change management, release governance, and reliability practices necessary to achieve enterprise-grade availability, scalability, security, compliance, and operational excellence.
