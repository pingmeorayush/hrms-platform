1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Document Management Module provides centralized storage, organization, access control, lifecycle management, and compliance governance for all organizational documents.
The module supports:
	•	Employee Documents
	•	Company Documents
	•	Policy Documents
	•	Contracts
	•	Digital Signatures
	•	Compliance Records
	•	Document Workflows

Business Objectives
	•	Centralize document storage
	•	Improve document discoverability
	•	Ensure compliance
	•	Reduce paper-based processes
	•	Improve security
	•	Support digital transformation

Conclusion
The Document Management Module provides a secure, scalable, and compliance-ready enterprise document platform that serves as the system of record for employee, payroll, legal, operational, and compliance documents.
The module integrates document storage, workflows, version control, digital signatures, retention management, OCR, AI-powered search, and compliance governance into a unified document ecosystem while maintaining auditability, security, scalability, and tenant isolation.
