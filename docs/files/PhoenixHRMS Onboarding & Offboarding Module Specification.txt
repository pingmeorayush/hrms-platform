1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Onboarding & Offboarding Module manages the complete employee lifecycle from offer acceptance through employee onboarding, probation, internal transitions, resignation, separation, and final exit.
The module provides:
	•	Preboarding
	•	Employee Onboarding
	•	Provisioning
	•	Induction Programs
	•	Probation Management
	•	Employee Exit Management
	•	Clearance Workflows
	•	Knowledge Transfer
	•	Asset Recovery
	•	Final Settlement Coordination

Business Objectives
	•	Accelerate employee productivity
	•	Standardize onboarding processes
	•	Improve employee experience
	•	Reduce onboarding delays
	•	Ensure compliant offboarding
	•	Protect organizational assets
	•	Improve workforce lifecycle visibility

Conclusion
The Onboarding & Offboarding Module provides a comprehensive employee lifecycle management platform that standardizes employee entry, transition, and exit processes across the organization.
The module integrates onboarding, probation, provisioning, clearance, knowledge transfer, and final settlement coordination into a unified lifecycle framework while maintaining compliance, auditability, security, scalability, and tenant isolation.
