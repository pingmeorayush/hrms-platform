Purpose
This document defines all business rules that govern PhoenixHRMS.
Business rules define:
	•	System behavior
	•	Validation requirements
	•	Workflow constraints
	•	Approval logic
	•	Compliance requirements
These rules are mandatory and must be enforced consistently throughout the platform.

Rule Categories
The platform contains the following rule groups:

BR-002 Attendance Rules
BR-003 Leave Rules

BR-001
Employee Management Rules

BR-EMP-001
Employee Code Uniqueness
Rule:
Every employee must possess a unique employee code.
Examples:
EMP00001
EMP00002
EMP00003
Validation:
Duplicate employee codes are prohibited.

BR-EMP-002
Employee Email Uniqueness
Rule:
An employee email address must be unique within an organization.
Validation:
System must reject duplicate email addresses.

BR-EMP-003
Mandatory Employee Fields
The following fields are mandatory:
	•	First Name
	•	Last Name
	•	Email
	•	Date of Joining
	•	Department
	•	Designation
System shall prevent employee creation if any mandatory field is missing.

BR-EMP-004
Termination Preservation
Rule:
Employee records shall never be physically deleted.
Instead:
Status = Terminated
Reason = Stored
Termination Date = Stored
Purpose:
Maintain compliance and historical records.

BR-EMP-005
Department Transfer History
Rule:
Department changes must create historical records.
System shall maintain:
	•	Previous Department
	•	New Department
	•	Effective Date
	•	Changed By

BR-002
Attendance Rules

BR-ATT-001
Single Check-In Rule
An employee may check in only once per working day.
Validation:
Second check-in attempts are rejected.
Exception:
Correction workflow.

BR-ATT-002
Check-Out Requirement
Employee must check in before check-out.
Validation:
Check-out without check-in is prohibited.

BR-ATT-003
Late Arrival Policy
Organization may define:
Grace Period
Example:
Shift Start: 09:00
Grace Period: 15 Minutes
Late Threshold:
09:16 onwards
Status:
Late

BR-ATT-004
Half-Day Calculation
Employee is marked Half Day when:
Worked Hours < Half-Day Threshold
Example:
Required Hours = 8
Half Day Threshold = 4
Worked Hours = 3.5
Result:
Half Day

BR-ATT-005
Overtime Eligibility
Overtime begins only after standard working hours are completed.
Example:
Required Hours = 8
Worked Hours = 10
Overtime = 2 Hours

BR-ATT-006
Attendance Lock
Attendance records become locked after payroll closure.
Purpose:
Prevent payroll discrepancies.

BR-003
Leave Rules

BR-LV-001
Leave Balance Validation
Employee cannot apply for leave exceeding available balance.

BR-LV-002
Leave Date Validation
Start Date cannot exceed End Date.

BR-LV-003
Overlapping Leave Restriction
Employee cannot submit overlapping leave requests.
Example:
Approved Leave
1 Jan – 5 Jan
New Request
4 Jan – 8 Jan
Rejected

BR-LV-004
Approval Workflow
Default Workflow:
Employee
↓
Manager
↓
HR
↓
Approved
Organizations may customize workflow.

BR-LV-005
Leave Cancellation
Pending requests may be cancelled.
Approved requests require cancellation approval.

BR-LV-006
Annual Leave Reset
Organizations may configure:
	•	Calendar Year Reset
	•	Financial Year Reset

BR-LV-007
Carry Forward
Carry-forward limits are configurable.
Example:
Maximum Carry Forward = 10 Days
Unused Leave = 18 Days
Carry Forward = 10 Days
Expired = 8 Days

BR-004
Payroll Rules

BR-PAY-001
Payroll Locking
Completed payroll periods become immutable.
No modification allowed.

BR-PAY-002
Payroll Frequency
Supported:
	•	Monthly
	•	Weekly
	•	Biweekly
Default:
Monthly

BR-PAY-003
Attendance Dependency
Payroll calculation requires finalized attendance.

BR-PAY-004
Leave Dependency
Payroll requires approved leave records.

BR-PAY-005
Salary Formula
Salary =
Basic Pay
	•	Allowances
	•	Bonus
− Deductions

BR-PAY-006
Loss Of Pay (LOP)
Unapproved absence may reduce salary.
Formula configurable.

BR-PAY-007
Mid-Month Joining
Salary prorated.
Example:
Joining Date = 16th
Payroll Days = 15
Salary = Prorated

BR-PAY-008
Mid-Month Exit
Salary calculated until final working day.

BR-PAY-009
Duplicate Payroll Prevention
Employee cannot receive duplicate payroll for same period.

BR-PAY-010
Payslip Generation
Every finalized payroll shall generate:
	•	PDF
	•	Printable Format
	•	Downloadable Format

BR-005
Recruitment Rules

BR-REC-001
Candidate Uniqueness
Candidate email shall be unique.

BR-REC-002
Resume Storage
Resume becomes immutable after submission.
Updates create versions.

BR-REC-003
Interview Evaluation
Every interview requires:
	•	Rating
	•	Feedback
	•	Recommendation

BR-REC-004
Offer Acceptance Deadline
Offer letters expire after configurable period.
Default:
7 Days

BR-006
Performance Rules

BR-PERF-001
Goal Ownership
Every goal requires an owner.

BR-PERF-002
Goal Weighting
Total weights must equal 100%.
Example:
Goal A = 40%
Goal B = 30%
Goal C = 30%
Total = 100%

BR-PERF-003
Review Finalization
Completed reviews become locked.
Changes require reopening workflow.

BR-007
Asset Rules

BR-ASSET-001
Unique Asset Tag
Every asset requires unique identifier.
Example:
LAP-0001
MON-0001

BR-ASSET-002
Single Assignment
Asset cannot be assigned to multiple employees simultaneously.

BR-ASSET-003
Exit Clearance
Employees must return assets before separation closure.

BR-008
Document Rules

BR-DOC-001
Document Encryption
All sensitive documents must be encrypted.

BR-DOC-002
Document Access
Users may access only authorized documents.

BR-DOC-003
Version Tracking
Updated documents create new versions.
Historical versions retained.

BR-009
Notification Rules

BR-NOTIF-001
Critical Notifications
Mandatory:
	•	Leave Approval
	•	Leave Rejection
	•	Payroll Completion
	•	Interview Scheduling

BR-NOTIF-002
Notification Retry
Failed notifications retried automatically.

BR-010
Security Rules

BR-SEC-001
Password Complexity
Minimum:
	•	12 Characters
	•	Uppercase
	•	Lowercase
	•	Number
	•	Special Character

BR-SEC-002
Session Timeout
Default:
30 Minutes
Configurable.

BR-SEC-003
Account Locking
5 failed attempts
↓
Temporary lock

BR-SEC-004
Multi-Factor Authentication
Supported:
	•	Email OTP
	•	Authenticator App

BR-011
Audit Rules

BR-AUD-001
Audit Coverage
The following actions must be audited:
	•	Employee Creation
	•	Employee Updates
	•	Leave Approval
	•	Payroll Processing
	•	Role Changes

BR-AUD-002
Immutable Audit Records
Audit logs cannot be modified.

BR-AUD-003
Audit Retention
Minimum retention:
7 Years
Configurable.

BR-012
AI Rules

BR-AI-001
AI Recommendations
AI recommendations shall never execute automatically.
Human approval required.

BR-AI-002
AI Transparency
Every recommendation must include explanation.
Example:
Leave Approval Recommendation
Reason:
Employee Leave Balance = 12
No conflicting requests

BR-AI-003
AI Confidence Score
Every prediction shall include confidence percentage.
Example:
Attrition Risk = 78%
Confidence = 92%

Compliance Rules
The platform shall support:
	•	GDPR
	•	ISO 27001
	•	SOC 2
	•	Local Labor Regulations
Where applicable.

Rule Precedence
When rules conflict:
	•	Legal Requirements
	•	Company Policies
	•	Payroll Rules
	•	Leave Rules
	•	System Defaults

Rule Change Management
Business rules are version controlled.
Every rule modification requires:
	•	Change Request
	•	Approval
	•	Audit Entry

Conclusion
This document defines the operational behavior of PhoenixHRMS.
All workflows, APIs, services, validations, UI forms, reports, and automation engines must strictly enforce these business rules.
Any behavior not covered by this document must be documented and approved before implementation.
