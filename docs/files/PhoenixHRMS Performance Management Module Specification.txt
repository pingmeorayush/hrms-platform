1. Module Overview
Purpose
The Performance Management Module helps organizations measure, manage, improve, and reward employee performance.
The module provides:
	•	Goal Management
	•	KPI Tracking
	•	Performance Reviews
	•	Continuous Feedback
	•	Competency Assessment
	•	Development Planning
	•	Talent Reviews
	•	Succession Planning

Business Objectives
	•	Improve employee performance
	•	Increase goal alignment
	•	Standardize performance reviews
	•	Support promotions and compensation decisions
	•	Identify future leaders
	•	Improve employee engagement

Conclusion
The Performance Management Module provides a comprehensive enterprise-grade talent performance platform that enables organizations to align strategy, measure outcomes, develop employees, identify future leaders, and drive workforce excellence.
The module integrates goals, reviews, competencies, feedback, development planning, talent reviews, and succession planning into a unified performance ecosystem while maintaining scalability, auditability, security, and tenant isolation.
