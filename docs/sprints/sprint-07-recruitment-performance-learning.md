# Sprint 07: Recruitment, Performance, and Learning

## Objective

Extend the platform from core HR operations into hiring, employee growth, and performance enablement.

## Primary Backlog IDs

- `REC-001`
- `PERF-001`
- `LMS-001`

## Module References

- [Recruitment](../modules/recruitment.md)
- [Performance Management](../modules/performance-management.md)
- [Learning Management](../modules/learning-management.md)

## Scope

- Recruitment from requisition through offer
- Candidate pipeline and interview coordination
- Goal setting and review-cycle basics
- Learning catalog, assignments, and progress tracking baseline

## Delivery Items

- Recruitment workflow and candidate-management APIs
- Interview and offer approval support
- Goals and review-cycle screens and services
- LMS catalog and assignment MVP

## Dependencies

- Sprints 01 and 02 for workflow, audit, tenant, and employee foundations
- Reporting baseline may be partially reused for funnel and review visibility

## Acceptance Criteria

- Recruiters can move candidates through a defined hiring pipeline
- Managers can create and run a basic review cycle
- Learning content can be assigned and progress can be tracked

## Test Focus

- Candidate state transitions
- Offer approval workflow
- Review submission and visibility rules
- Learning assignment and completion tracking

## Risks and Open Questions

- Recruitment and learning specs exist partly in PDF-only source material and may need deeper refinement later
- Combining three large talent areas in one sprint may require strict MVP slicing
